Full Belly Farm

Getting there

Full Belly Farm is situated along the Cache Creek river on Highway 16. Most of you will get there by taking 505 North off of 80. If you can get to the Cache Creek Casino then it's only a few minutes up the road.

 

Ceremony & Reception

The ceremony starts at 4:30PM amidst a beautiful grove of walnut trees. Bring comfortable shoes that you can walk with on slightly uneven ground.

Reception, dinner and merriment to follow the ceremony.

Lodging

Free Camping!

Bring your tent and camp among the trees for free. Breakfast the next morning will be included.

Hampton Inn @ Woodland, CA

Mention you are part of the "Kirkland wedding" while booking to get a nice discount. Includes a shuttle to/from the Sacramento Airport w/ advance reservation. Woodland is about 40 min from Guinda.

Capay Valley Bed and Breakfast (near Guinda)

Capay Valley Bed and Breakfast has a cozy selection of rooms, cottages and shared areas offers you plenty of home comforts; set in the most beautiful surroundings.
